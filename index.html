Welcome to cat world

A cat is a small domesticated mammal scientifically known as Felis catus.

Key points:

  • Family: Felidae (The same family as lions, tigers and other big cats).
  • Size: Usually small, weighing 5-20 pounds (2-9kg).
  • Lifespan: 12-15 years on average (some live 20+).
  • Diet: Carnivorous (Mostle meat-eaters).
  • Behavior:
    • Often independent but can form strong bonds with humans.
    • Known for grooming themselves, hunting instincts and purring.
    • Active mainly at dawn and dusk (crepuscular).
